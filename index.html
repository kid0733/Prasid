ASSOCIATES.

Chyangra Pashmina

We also offer you “CHYANGRA PASHMINA” labels for our 100% Cashmere products, which is a certification of the highest quality of Cashmere products. Chyangra Pashmina is the highest grade, finest cashmere in the world featuring the rare fibers of the Chyangra mountain goats of Himalayas. Inspired by the Nepali culture and tradition, Chyangra Pashmina products are created with environmentally-friendly and ethical handmade production processes.

Nepal Pashmina Industries Association

As an associate company of the NPIA, we are committed to supporting the NPIA's mission to promote the Pashmina industry in Nepal. We believe that the Pashmina industry is a valuable asset to Nepal and that it has the potential to generate significant economic benefits for the country. We are committed to working with the NPIA to promote the Pashmina industry and to ensure that it is a success.

Federation of Handicraft Associations of Nepal

As an associate company of the Federation of Handicraft Associations of Nepal, we are committed to supporting the Federation's mission to promote and preserve the handicrafts of Nepal. We believe that the handicrafts of Nepal are a valuable asset to the country and that they have the potential to generate significant economic benefits. We are committed to working with the Federation to promote the handicrafts of Nepal and to ensure that they are a success.
